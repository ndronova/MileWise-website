Let's help small businesses grow!

Small businesses are what makes our community special. What would your suburb be without a small barber shop, a local restaurant with amazing food and warm, welcoming atmosphere or a farmers shop with always fresh produce?

These places hold a special place in our hearts because they care deeply about us.

CharityStreet helps users discover small local places that will otherwise be missed and offer discounts and extra "points" that will later be converted into donations

CharityStreet is here to help local businesses grow and increase their brand awareness. Our app features:


Local restaurants

Coffee shops

Small shops

Sports and wellness places

Small grocery and deli shops
